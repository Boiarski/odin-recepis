Morrocan Couscous

Vegetables marrocan couscous

This is a light, fresh and easy meal, quick to make and goes along very well with chicken and sour cream. It oozes summer vibes.

Ingridients

  • Couscous - 500 grams
  • Carrot - 1 large size
  • Leek - 1 large stem
  • Onion - 1 midium size
  • Garlic - 5 large cloves
  • Sicilian lemon - 1 large size
  • Cashew or Brazil nut - 100 grams
  • Parsiley - to taste
  • Mint - to taste
  • Butter - to taste
  • Olive oil - to taste
  • Sal - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste
